About us

Flowers prepared with care for meaningful gifting.

Fleur en Fleur is a floral store in Surakarta (Solo) that focuses on made-by-request arrangements for special occasions and personal moments.

Our story

Designed to feel personal, elegant, and expressive.

Fleur en Fleur offers bouquets, garden flowers, bloomboxes, and vase arrangements that can be adjusted by size, flower type, mood, and color board. The goal is to help customers send flowers that match the exact feeling of the occasion.

Whether the order is romantic, celebratory, comforting, or formal, the shop is built around custom discussion and thoughtful floral styling.

Why customers choose us

Simple ordering and custom floral direction.

Special occasion focus

Created for birthdays, graduations, anniversaries, sympathy, house warming, new baby, and grand openings.

Custom request friendly

Multiple presentation styles
